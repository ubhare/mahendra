What we do

We offer different types of services under one roof.

Export

We are exporters & suppliers of branded & generic medicines from India. Our product range includes oncology, HIV medicines & other life saving drugs like generic Sofosbuvir - MyHep, Hepcinat. Oncology products like Geftinib - Geftinat, Imatinib - Imatib, Abiraterone - Zelgor, Zecyte, Chlorambucil - Clokeran, Erlotinib -Erlonat, HIV products like Viraday, Tenvir EM, Isentress, Duovir etc.. We are exporters of pharmaceutical finished formulations. Our products are manufactured by WHO-GMP companies which include GSK, Pfizer, MSD, Sun Pharma, Cipla, Natco Pharma etc.

Medicare for Self

We provide medicines on regular basis so you don't have to miss your appropriate dose. Just fill in the form and we will take care of your medicines which will be of best brand and hence safe and have large variety so you do not have to run helter and skeltor for medicine. Remember! you just have to fill our form, That's all.

Dispensing

If you want prescribed medicines by your doctors. We can deliver it to your doorstep. You just have to email or whatsapp your prescription and we will keep ready your medicines ready for pick up so that you will get at your doorstep.
